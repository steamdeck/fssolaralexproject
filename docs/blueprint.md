# **App Name**: Solaris Hub

## Core Features:

- Homepage: Display an engaging overview of the company, mission, and key benefits of solar energy.
- About Us Page: Show the company history, values, team members, and expertise in solar solutions.
- Our Services Page: Detail the range of solar services offered, including consultation, installation, maintenance, and financing options.
- Contact Us Page: Allow users to submit inquiries via a contact form, find contact information, and view a map of the location.
- Savings Calculator: Estimate potential savings and environmental impact with solar panel using generative AI as a tool
- Portfolio Showcase: Feature examples of successfully installed solar panel projects with images and customer testimonials.
- Blog/News Section: Post articles, news, and updates related to solar energy, industry trends, and company achievements.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant blue (#2E90FA) to evoke trust, reliability, and the sky, symbolizing solar energy.
- Background color: Light blue (#EBF4FF), offering a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Yellow (#FDD03E), reminiscent of the sun and solar power, used to highlight key information and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for a modern and clean user interface.
- Use clean, minimalist icons representing solar panels, energy efficiency, and environmental benefits.
- Emphasize a clear, grid-based layout to present information in an easily digestible format, with prominent calls-to-action.
- Incorporate subtle animations on hover effects and transitions to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
